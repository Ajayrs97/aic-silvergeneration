Introduction to MediSave Care  

--------------------------------

MediSave Care is a long-term care scheme that was launched on 1 October 2020. It allows Singapore Citizens or Permanent Residents aged 30 and above who are severely disabled to make monthly cash withdrawals up to a total of $200 per month from their own and/or their spouseâ€™s MediSave Accounts for their long-term care needs.

For more info, click [here](https://www.aic.sg/financial-assistance/medisave-care)

For the list of assessors, click [here](https://www.aic.sg/Assessors-list)

For submission of application, click [eFASS](https://efinance.aic.sg/)
